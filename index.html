The History of Mario

Mario is a fictional video game character created by Shigeru Miyamoto for the video game company, Nintendo. Mario's first ever appearance in a video game was Donkey Kong; which was released in 1981 for arcade machines.


Mario has been in many games to date, but here are a few that are super memorable.

Super Mario Bros 1985
Super Mario World 1990
Super Smash Bros 1999
Super Mario Galaxy 2007

Through the years, Mario has had many different friends by his side. Here are just a few that are also major staples in the Mario franchise.

  • His brother Luigi
  • Princess Peach
  • Toad
  • Yoshi the dinosaur
