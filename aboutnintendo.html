About Nintendo

Founded on September 23, 1889, by Fusajiro Yamauchi, it originally produced handmade hanafuda playing cards. By 1963, the company had tried several small niche businesses, such as cab services and love hotels.

Nintendo abandoned previous ventures in toys in 1960, and developed into a video game company in the 1970s. Nintendo is now one of the largest video game company worldwide.

Nintendo's first hand-held game system, the Game and Watch was a huge success, which helped boost Nintendo's presence in the video game industry world wide.
