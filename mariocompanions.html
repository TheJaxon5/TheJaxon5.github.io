Mario's companions

Luigi

Meet Luigi. This is Mario's younger brother. The under dog. Luigi looks very similar to Mario, but has many features that easily identifies him.
He is green of course, taller, he jumps higher, and if he flaps is legs around in the air in the games he floats. Luigi has always been second to Mario, and has many less games.
Luigi doesn't have many solo games, but a few consist of: Luigi's Mansion, New Super Luigi Bros, and Dr Luigi. All of these games were released during the year of Luigi (March 2013- March 2014).
Luigi also had parts as playable characters in alol the Mario Kart games, and also the Super Smahs Bros games.
